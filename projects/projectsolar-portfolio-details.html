Project Overview:

Introduction: The project addresses the imperative shift towards green, renewable energy, with a specific focus on the consistent and reliable nature of solar energy. Acknowledging its potential to reduce utility bills and contribute to energy independence, the initiative aims to provide an easy and efficient pathway for customers to adopt solar energy. The central project management portal serves as the cornerstone for connecting expertise, facilitating collaboration among sales, engineering, management teams, and customers.

Purpose: With only 3% of energy needs met by solar in the US, the project aims to expedite the adoption of solar energy. The portal streamlines the implementation process by connecting customers with expert engineers and vendors, creating a seamless journey from sales consultation to project completion. The end goal is to empower customers to transition to solar energy easily and efficiently.

Approach: The project adopts the Scrum framework, a flexible Agile project management approach, emphasizing iterative and incremental development. The Scrum framework divides the project into manageable user stories, planned in sprints. The roles of Product Owner, Scrum Master, and Development team collaborate to ensure efficient project delivery. The team formed a cross-functional unit, defined a vision, and executed sprints, conducting regular standups, sprint reviews, and retrospectives. The project commenced on February 22, 2023, with meticulous planning and continuous improvement throughout the development process.

In summary, the project's approach combines the benefits of solar energy with the efficiency of the Scrum framework, providing a comprehensive solution for customers looking to make the transition to solar power.
